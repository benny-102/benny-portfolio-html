My foundation is a BSc in Physiology from the University of Ibadan, where I developed a precise, systems-level way of thinking about complex biological processes. That same thinking now drives how I approach machine learning: understand the system, find the signal, build something that works.

I have built and deployed AI systems spanning NLP, anomaly detection, RAG, and conversational AI. My most recognised work includes a fine-tuned BERT-based mental health detection API reaching 92% classification accuracy and a fraud detection system processing 195,000+ transactions using Isolation Forest with engineered features.

I care about AI that solves problems at the system level, not just models that benchmark well in isolation.
